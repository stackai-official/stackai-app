This Privacy Policy describes how RestoDoc ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the RestoDoc mobile application ("the App"). RestoDoc is a field documentation app for property restoration professionals. The app allows users to manage restoration jobs, capture photos and videos, log moisture readings, track drying equipment, sketch floor plans, and generate AI-powered reports.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — When you create an account, we collect your email address and password.
  • Job details — Information you provide when creating restoration jobs, including property addresses, loss type, insurance claim details, adjuster contact information, and notes.
  • Photos and videos — Media you capture through the App to document job sites, which are uploaded to our secure cloud storage.
  • Moisture readings — Data you enter including reading values, locations, materials, and dates.
  • Equipment logs — Records of drying equipment placed on job sites, including type, quantity, serial numbers, and placement dates.
  • Floor plans — Sketches and annotations you create within the App.
  • Device identifier — A randomly generated anonymous device ID is stored locally on your device to maintain your session. This ID is not linked to your personal identity.
  • Usage data — Basic information about how you interact with the App, such as which screens you visit, used to improve the App experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality for job documentation
  • Store and organize your restoration job data, photos, and readings
  • Generate AI-powered damage assessment reports using your job data
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer service
  • Maintain your session and preferences on your device
  • Improve our App and services

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers — We use Supabase for secure data storage and authentication, and Anthropic's Claude AI for report generation. These providers process data only as necessary to operate our App.
  • AI processing — When you generate a report, your job data (excluding photos) is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI to produce the report narrative. This data is not retained by Anthropic after processing.
  • Legal requirements — We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal process.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your personal information is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider with industry-standard encryption. Photos and videos are stored in Supabase Storage with access controls. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Row Level Security (RLS) ensures you can only access your own data.

5. Push Notifications

If you grant permission, the App may send push notifications for job updates and reminders. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

6. Your Choices

You may:

  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data via our account deletion page
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings
  • Delete individual jobs, photos, readings, and equipment records within the App
  • Clear your locally stored session data by signing out or uninstalling the App

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
